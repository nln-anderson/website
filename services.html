How It Works...

I offer both in-person (San Diego) and online tutoring. By clicking "Book Now", you can schedule an online tutoring session. An email will be sent with a meeting link. The meeting is via Google Meet, so a Google account is necessary (it is free). You will be able to join the meeting at the time it is scheduled. I use a digital whiteboard that the students and I can write on, that way the lesson is more interactive. I recommend a Microsoft account to make whiteboard sharing easier.

For assemblying a tutoring plan or for in-person tutoring, please reach out to me via phone or email. I would love to hear what you are looking for and how I could help!
